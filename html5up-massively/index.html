About Us

The Owners

We inherited this laundrette to bring a new and fresh way to think about doing laundry. We wanted to create a fun and friendly atmosphere for people to come and socialise while doing their laundry. With our renovations finally completed we can do just that!

Our new hipster laundrette is a place for everyone to come and enjoy a cup of coffee or a bite to eat while doing laundry. We specialise in cookies which are freshly made in shop each day, we also do personalised cookies for gifts or to be enjoyed in the cafe.
